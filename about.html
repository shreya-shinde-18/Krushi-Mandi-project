About Krushi Mandi

Empowering Agriculture Through Technology

Our Mission

Krushi Mandi is dedicated to revolutionizing the agricultural marketplace by connecting farmers directly with buyers, eliminating unnecessary middlemen and ensuring fair prices for both parties.

We believe that farmers deserve the best value for their hard work, and buyers deserve access to fresh, quality produce at competitive prices. Our platform makes this possible through innovative technology and transparent trading practices.

🎯 Our Vision

To become India's most trusted agricultural marketplace, empowering millions of farmers and creating sustainable livelihoods.

💡 Innovation

Leveraging technology to bring transparency, efficiency, and fairness to agricultural trading.

🤝 Community

Building a strong community of farmers, buyers, and businessmen working together for mutual growth.

What We Offer

📊 Smart Pricing System

Compare prices across multiple mandis to make informed decisions and get the best deals.

🔒 Secure Platform

Safe and secure transactions with role-based access for different user types.

📱 Easy to Use

Simple, intuitive interface designed for users of all technical backgrounds.

🚚 Direct Delivery

Arrange direct delivery from farmers to buyers, reducing costs and ensuring freshness.

💬 Request Management

Farmers can submit sell requests and receive offers from multiple buyers and businessmen.

📈 Market Insights

Access real-time market trends and pricing information to make better business decisions.
